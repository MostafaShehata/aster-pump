AsterPump X17 Maintenance Policy

The filter cassette must be replaced every 19 days, not monthly. The replacement date must be written on the green service tag.

Calibration cycle C2 must be run after replacing the silver impeller sensor, moving the unit more than 5 meters, or updating firmware.

The coolant loop should be inspected every 11 days. If the return valve shows residue, drain 200 ml of coolant before restarting the pressure monitor.

Only Level 2 technicians may approve operation after repeated E-77 pressure echo events. Level 1 operators may perform standard filter cassette replacement.
